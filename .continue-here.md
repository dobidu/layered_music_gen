## Critical findings that changed scope (do not lose)

Two real bugs in `music_gen.py` were confirmed by grep during research and are now requirements for Phase 1:

1. **P-A — Arrangement re-roll bug.** `mix_and_save` calls `generate_song_arrangement()` at `music_gen.py:760` after MIDI has already been generated. Any random draws consumed inside that call shift downstream soundfont/FX RNG, breaking reproducibility and potentially making MIDI and audio describe different structures. → R-S3.

2. **P-B — pydub gain/pan is a no-op.** `music_gen.py:845-852` assigns to the read-only `.volume` property and discards the return of `.pan()`. `levels.json` has zero effect on any current output. Fixing this will change audio for every existing seed. → R-S4.

Other research-driven additions:
- Only new runtime dep: `typer>=0.12`. `python-json-logger` is **already** in `requirements.txt`.
- Determinism contract: MIDI + metadata always bit-identical; audio bit-identical only under pinned FluidSynth binary.
- `beat_anotator.py` generates theoretical straight-grid beats that drift with swing > 0 — replace with MIDI-tick-derived beats (`beats.py` in Phase 4).
